About Us

Our vision is to to keep Canada at the forefront of emerging blockchain and decentralized technologies. And, our mission is to connect Canadian blockchain conversations, research, and development. Below are our 4 core values:

Leadership

Blockchain Canada was founded on the premise that blockchains have the power, potential, and promise to transform many aspects of Canada’s financial, social, and governance systems in ways that make them more decentralized, open, and equitable.

Transformation

Such transformations, however, will affect all Canadians and its important to ensure that all classes of stakeholders (private, public regulatory, academic, and more) are connected in open conversations about how use this science to benefit Canadians and build Canada as leader in blockchain technology.

Unity

Conversations about blockchains and their role in Canada are happening, but often occur in silos and disconnected communities. Let’s connect them by addressing some underlying questions such as who is doing innovative work on blockchain tech in Canada?

Visbility

Other questions revolve around its use, progress, and ramifications in relation to academia, business, politics, society, and the like. As this not-for-profit grows and brings more partners to the table those questions and issues will be addressed. Join us to help share information and make Canada a great blockchain ecosystem.
